About Us!

Welcome to Parry Farms, LLC! We are a family-owned farm dedicated to raising happy and healthy goats. Our mission is to provide high-quality products while ensuring the well-being of our animals.
At Parry Farms, we believe in bringing quality products to our community. We take pride in our commitment to animal welfare and the quality of our products.
The majority of our soaps & lotions are made with sheeps milk becuase of the additional vitamins and minerals. If you are interested in purchasing sheep's milk or other sheep products, please visit our friends at Sheep Mountain Creamery. Thank you for visiting our website! We hope you enjoy learning more about us and our farm!
